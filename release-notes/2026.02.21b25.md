Expose structure-level guest access, schedule, and pincode traits that were present in the v2 stream but dropped by the parser.

## Highlights
- Retain and decode `nest.trait.user.UserAccessTrait` from the v2 observe stream.
- Retain and decode `weave.trait.schedule.BasicUserSchedulesSettingsTrait` from the v2 observe stream.
- Surface structure-level `weave.trait.security.UserPincodesSettingsTrait` in `all_traits` and diagnostics instead of suppressing it.
- Add direct protobuf-handler unit coverage for the new manual decoders and allowlist path.

## Why
- `2026.02.21b24` logs proved the raw Nest stream already includes `user_access_records`, `basic_structure_pincode_schedules_settings`, and structure-level `user_pincodes`.
- The integration was still normalizing only `GuestsTrait` and `ApplicationKeysTrait`, which hid the strongest remaining passcode-adjacent evidence.
